# Community Resources

A curated list of free resources, PDFs, and videos to accelerate your Go learning journey. Found something good? You can contribute by clicking the **Edit this page on GitHub** link at the bottom.

## Free Books and Guides

*   [Gophercises](<https://gophercises.com/>) - A free coding course for Go developers that teaches you how to build mini applications.
*   [Learn Go with Tests](<https://quii.gitbook.io/learn-go-with-tests/>) - Learn Go and Test Driven Development (TDD) by writing tests.
*   [Effective Go](<https://go.dev/doc/effective_go>) - The official guide to writing clear, idiomatic Go code.
